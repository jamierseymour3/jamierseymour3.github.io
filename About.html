CY$EC

CY$EC is the world leader in free Cyber security infromation.

CY$EC is a non profit initiative started by the FBI in order to provide members of the human race with the tools to protect themselves during the impending alien cyber warfare.

We beleive that winning the war on cyber security will cement our place as the most advanced planet in our solar system, the martians deployment of top hackers poses a huge threat to our computer security.
It is thus of growning importance to arm yourself with the right knowledge in order to keep yourself safe. This website was created as a guide to make cyber security easy for everyone!
