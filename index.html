What is Cyber Security?

Cyber Security is the protection of computing systems and the data that they store or access.

Cyber security or Computer includes controlling physical access to the hardware, as well as protecting against harm that may come via network access, data and code injection.

The field is of growing importance due to the increasing reliance on computer systems and the Internet, wireless networks such as Bluetooth and Wi-Fi, the growth of "smart" devices.
It is thus of growning importance to arm yourself with the right knowledge in order to keep yourself safe. This website was created as a guide to make cyber security easy for everyone!
