CYBER SECURITY

Cyber Security is the protection of computing systems and the data that they store or access.

Cyber Security refers to the body of technologies, processes and practices designed to protect networks, computers, programs and data from attack, damage or unauthorized access.

The field is of growing importance due to the increasing reliance on computer systems and the Internet, wireless networks such as Bluetooth and Wi-Fi, the growth of "smart" devices.
Furthermore ensuring Cyber Security has never been simple.It is already a complex subject ansd because attacks evolve every day as attackers become more inventive it is very improtant to be able to define cyber security and identify what constitutes good cyber security. This is important beacuse Cyber Security costs worldwide continue to grow every year, and are expected to reach 101 billion by 2018.

This is why we have created an easy ten step list to ensure you and your computer are kept safe during these dark times:

1. Establish strong passwords
Implementing strong passwords is the easiest thing you can do to strengthen your security.

2. Put up a strong firewall
In order to have a properly protected network, a firewall is of utmost importance.

3. Install antivirus protection
Antivirus and anti-malware software are essentials in your arsenal of online security weapons, as well.

4. Update your programs regularly.
This is an essential step towards full protection.

Smart phones these days hold almost as much valueable data as computers, therfore securing them is a must.
